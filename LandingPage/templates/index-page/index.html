Message from the University President

Welcome AACCUP accreditors!

On behalf of our community at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines let me thank you, first and foremost, for your untiring efforts to ensuring the culture of quality in higher education institutions. PUP is more than grateful to have been assisted by your expert accreditors throughout these years. We are always one with you in keeping with national and global standards in education for the benefit of our nation.


I am excited about this endevaour for this will surely bring us to another level of progress. This institutional accreditation is a critical step for us to ensure that we are always at our best and is bringing only the best for all our students. We also believe that quality education necessitates continuous evaluation. Without it, we can never truly achieve our objectives of becoming the first National Polytechnic University.


We, at the PUP community, will continue to work together to deliver timely and relevant knowledge. While we have been disrupted by the pandemic situation, we will remain steadfast. With the concerted effort of all university officials, faculty members, and employees, we commit to staying resilient. We will continue to rise to the challenges with fervor to truly serve all Iskolar ng bayan.


We owe it to all PUPians and the rest of the nation whatever success we reap from this endevaour. With this, I express my ceaseless support and prayers to all of you in the conduct of this institutional accreditation. I wish an empowered and purposeful journey as we all lead the University at its most excellent.


Mula sayo, para sa Bayan.
